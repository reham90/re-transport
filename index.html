Aviation Oils

Forsage Avia oils are manufactured from 100% solvent-refined mineral base stock and intended for reciprocating engines, gas-turbine engines, helicopter gearboxes and pitch hinges. These oils are also used in oil blends for the lubrication of turboprop engines, helicopter pitch hinges and gas-compressor engines

how we work

01

you place an order

If you want to place an order or ask a question, you should fill in our online form or request a callback.

02

OUR MANAGER CONTACTS YOU

After processing your order or callback request, we will contact you to discuss individual terms of shipment and your personal discount.

03

WE NEGOTIATE SHIPMENT TERMS

At this stage, we negotiate the scope of supply, type of packing, special terms of shipment and delivery dates. A purchase and sale contract is made to record all the arrangements between the parties.

04

YOU PAY FOR THE GOODS

Based on your order and terms of the contract, our manager issues an invoice for you to make a payment for the goods.

05

WE SHIP THE GOODS

Once we receive your payment, we proceed to manufacturing and packaging the goods to be transported to your warehouse subject to the contract.

06

YOU RECEIVE

Excellent products and a reliable partner. We guarantee the highest quality of lubricants, first-class customer services and the best price.
